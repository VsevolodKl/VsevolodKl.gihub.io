PRAYING GENTLY

the song was written and produced by Headment in 2026

ABOUT

Everyday you enter this beautiful world of beauty & bullshit. You search & search & search, fear that you missed sth. You wanna be a part, but feel so small. At this point many just start this big talk of how evil the internet is, but the only thing that's wrong is that joy became a habit. Not a choice.

But how to find the exit? Stop searching for it.

—From My Own Experience

"First lines came up in the spring of 2023. There was a line saying repeatedly: "I hope u'll find it quickly" But i was pretty steady, feeling like i could make sth huge out of it.
October 2025. I just turned 18. I was fucking lost. The whole life ur crushing ur skull for shiny gold medals. Ur head is aching, ur body's sore, but ur still wasting ur time searching for THE BEST words, THE BEST notes, takes, days, meanings & shit. But life is so fucking short, and your song is only 4 minutes long.

Get things done. Move.
That's how you deal with perfection"

—On How The Song Was Made
